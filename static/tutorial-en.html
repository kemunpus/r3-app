1. Introduction

  • Thank you for your cooperation on the experiment conducted by Hirose’s project members in Advanced Institute of Industrial Technology.
  • In this experiment, we shall utilize only the results, such as your input words, sentences and the number of your answers. Please note that we shall not exploit your personal information, like name, address, age, sex, religion, policy, ethics, email address, cookies, ISP and IP address.
  • Although the experimental results is disclosed to third party, the individuals who provide some answers in this experiment are not identified.
  • If you want to delete your input information during this experiment, please contact us (see our email address described at the bottom) and inform us of “date of experiment” and “information that you want to delete”. Then, we will remove the information.
  • Please let us know it if you have some question. We will reply to your question within 3 business days. When you do not receive any response in 3 business days, please contact us again.
  • When you cannot attend this experiment, you can close your browser.

2. Supported browsers

  • You can attend this experiment by modern PC browsers like Chrome, Firefox and Edge. Internet Explorer cannot be used due to the technical reasons. It is recommended to use PC instead of mobile devices from stability point of view.
  • Since this experiment requires the download of videos and images, high-speed internet connection is also reccommended. Please note that packets will be consumed if you are on 'pay-as-you-go' network.
  • Although this system displays the descriptions in English, you can use another languages supported by your operating-system. The experiment host will inform you acceptable languages.

3. Experiment

  • This experiment consists of the following steps.

Step 1: Input information

  • First, input “Room” that is information for grouping the participants of the experiment. The host of experiment would inform you of the “Room” information.
  • Next, input “Nickname” which represents the information to distinguish the participants of experiments. System will generate an 'anonymous' name automatically if you not specified.
  • Finally, select “Logic” that corresponds to the contents of experiment. The host of experiment would inform you of the “Logic” information.
  • When you are ready, click to “Start”.

Step 2: Check the content of experiment

  • Read the task and duration of experiment.
  • When you are ready, click to “Start”.

Step 3: Conduct an experiment

  • Conduct the mentioned task by watching some support such as texts and vedeos.
  • Input each answer in each line.
  • After the experiment is terminated, you can automatically proceed with step 4.

Step 4: Check your input information

  • Click to “Done” after you check the information input during the experiment.
  • That’s all. Thank you very much!
